I am a Computer Science MSc student at [EPFL](https://www.epfl.ch/) (GPA: 5.44/6), focusing on Machine Learning, Foundation Models, NLP, and Reinforcement Learning. I hold a BSc in Telecommunications Engineering from [UPV](https://www.upv.es/) (GPA: 9/10, Top 3/200+).

Currently, I work as a **Machine Learning Engineer at Xcert AI**, where I raised LLM QA accuracy from 35% to 95% by building hybrid semantic search and graph-based RAG over aerospace compliance data, and cut PDF processing latency by 10x.

Previously, I worked as an **RL Researcher at Instituto de Telecomunicaciones**, improving UAV base-station positioning by 71-97% over baselines using PPO with continuous actions, and as an **NLP Researcher at the Computer Vision & Behaviour Analysis Lab**, achieving 78.3% multilabel emotion accuracy by fine-tuning Llama-2 7B with QLoRA.

My research interests include **adversarial robustness of generative models**, **deep reinforcement learning for telecommunications**, and **large language models**.
