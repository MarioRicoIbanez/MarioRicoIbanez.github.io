publications

  1. Optimizing UAV Aerial Base Station Flights Using DRL-based Proximal Policy Optimization
    Mario Rico Ibáñez, Azim Akhtarshenas, David López-Pérez, and 1 more author
    In IEEE International Conference on Communications (ICC), 2025
    Abs arXiv Bib

    UAV-based base stations offer a promising solution in emergencies where the rapid deployment of cutting-edge networks is crucial for maximizing life-saving potential. This paper introduces an automated reinforcement learning approach that enables UAVs to dynamically interact with their environment and determine optimal configurations. Leveraging radio signal sensing capabilities of communication networks, we utilize the proximal policy optimization algorithm to learn and generalize positioning strategies across diverse user equipment movement patterns.

  2. Mejorando el Análisis de Emociones en texto mediante Llama 2
    Mario Rico Ibáñez, Rocío Amor, and Valery Naranjo
    In XLI Congreso Anual de la Sociedad Española de Ingeniería Biomédica, 2023
    Abs Bib

    Mental health is crucial in today’s society. This paper presents a novel perspective on emotion analysis using the Llama 2 model to effectively classify emotions in formal datasets, demonstrating how Llama 2 outperforms conventional approaches for multilabel emotion recognition.
