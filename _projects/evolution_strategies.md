**Abstract**

Investigated **Evolution Strategies** as standalone RL methods and pretraining mechanisms for gradient-based algorithms (DQN, PPO). Conducted extensive experiments across **Flappy Bird**, **Breakout**, and **MuJoCo** environments with discrete and continuous action spaces. Analyzed **trade-offs** between **gradient-free** and **gradient-based methods** in terms of convergence speed, final performance, and hyperparameter sensitivity. Demonstrated that ES provides effective early exploration in simple environments but faces scalability limitations in high-dimensional tasks.

Collaborative project with [Swiss Data Science Center](https://www.datascience.ch/).
