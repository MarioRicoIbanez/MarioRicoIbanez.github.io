**Feb 2025 -- Jun 2025 | EPFL, LIONS Lab**

Investigated the robustness of image-to-image generative models against adversarial attacks (e.g. Projected Gradient Descent). Developed adversarial strategies for diffusion-based architectures and analyzed defense mechanisms such as RobustCLIP. Conducted large-scale experiments to evaluate performance degradation and model stability under adversarial perturbations.

- Reduced CLIP similarity from 0.88 to 0.74 (16%) under APGD attacks across 3 diffusion models
- Evaluated 10k+ generated images; analyzed RobustCLIP & dataset filtering trade-offs
- Supervised by Prof. Volkan Cevher and Elias Abad Rocamora

**Skills:** Deep Learning, Generative Models, Adversarial Robustness, PyTorch
