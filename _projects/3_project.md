**Feb 2025 | EPFL**

Developed an AI tutoring system by fine-tuning Qwen3-0.6B-Base for STEM-related questions at EPFL. Implemented supervised fine-tuning, quantization, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), and direct preference optimization (DPO). Built a custom FAISS-based retrieval system for EPFL course materials, achieving strong performance in benchmarks such as MMLU and MathQA.

- Improved STEM QA accuracy from 0.25 to 0.40 via 3-stage curriculum, SFT + RAG + quantization
- Indexed 39k chunks in FAISS for efficient retrieval
- Applied DPO to boost reasoning (0.841) rivaling larger models

**Skills:** Natural Language Processing, Large Language Models, RAG
