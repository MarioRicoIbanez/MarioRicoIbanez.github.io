**Jan 2024 -- Jun 2025 | Instituto de Telecomunicaciones y Aplicaciones Multimedia**

Applied Deep Reinforcement Learning for optimizing aerial base station positioning in emergency communication networks. Developed and evaluated DRL algorithms for 3D UAV positioning across multiple mobility scenarios.

- Improved UAV base-station positioning by 71-97% over baselines across 6 scenarios using PPO with continuous actions and signal-based states
- Scaled training to 12,000+ episodes with a GPU-accelerated 5G/6G simulator (massive MIMO)
- Retained 91% performance under high AoA noise (STD=100°)
- Published on [arXiv (2504.03961)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.03961)

**Skills:** Deep Reinforcement Learning, Telecommunications, PyTorch
