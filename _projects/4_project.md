**Sep 2024 -- Jan 2025 | EPFL, LTS4 Lab**

Investigating, supervised by the LTS4 lab for the AI4Science project for CS-433ML course, the impact of magnification in H&E-stained TCGA whole-slide image datasets (5x, 10x, and 20x) on the embeddings generated by digital pathology foundation models.

- Systematically evaluated UNI foundation model robustness across multiple magnifications
- Used three breast histology datasets (BACH, BRACS, BreakHis)
- Showed magnification shifts influence feature quality despite maintained performance

**Skills:** Computer Vision, Foundation Models, Computational Pathology
