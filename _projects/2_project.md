**Feb 2025 -- Jun 2025 | EPFL, Swiss Data Science Center**

Explored Evolution Strategies (ES) as reinforcement learning algorithms and as pretraining methods for gradient-based approaches (DQN, PPO). Ran extensive experiments in Flappy Bird, Breakout, and MuJoCo environments. Analyzed convergence speed, scalability, and performance trade-offs.

- Implemented ES-pretrained agents across multiple environments
- Compared ES pretraining with random initialization for DQN and PPO
- Published comprehensive comparative analysis with open-source implementation

**Skills:** Reinforcement Learning, Evolution Strategies, PyTorch
