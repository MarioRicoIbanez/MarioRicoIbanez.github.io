**Abstract**

Investigated the robustness of **Image-to-Image generative models** against **adversarial attacks**, such as **Automatic Projected Gradient Descent**, analyzing vulnerabilities and defenses, such as **RobustCLIP**. Developed adversarial strategies targeting models based on **Diffusion Models**, specifically assessing the stability of embeddings. Conducted extensive experiments evaluating the performance impacts of adversarial perturbations across datasets.

Supervised by **Prof. Volkan Cevher** and **Elias Abad Rocamora**.
